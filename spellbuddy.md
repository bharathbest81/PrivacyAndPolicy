Privacy Policy for SpellBuddy
Last Updated: February 2026
1. Introduction
SpellBuddy ("we," "our," or "the App") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of children and all users. This policy explains how information is handled.
2. Data Collection & Usage
Local Storage: Data is stored only on the user's mobile device.
No External Transmission: Personal data is not collected, stored, or transmitted to servers or any third parties.
OCR Images: Images captured by the camera for word recognition are processed locally and are not stored or shared.
3. Advertising
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To comply with the Google Play Families Policy:
All ad requests are flagged as Child-Directed (COPPA/GDPR-K).
Ad content is restricted to a "G" (General Audience) rating.
Personalized advertising or tracking is not performed.
